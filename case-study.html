Description

This project focused on creating a dynamic website for my college's Industry Night, an event showcasing the final-term portfolios and projects of students to a wide audience, including industry professionals and potential employers. I collaborated with a team of four to design, develop, and implement a platform that seamlessly presented students' work, while providing essential event details and ensuring a smooth user experience for a large number of guests.

Challenge

The main challenge was to develop a site that could effectively display diverse multimedia projects while accommodating high traffic during the event. The site needed to cater to various user needs, from browsing student portfolios to accessing event schedules and venue information. Additionally, we aimed to create an intuitive design that was both visually appealing and easy to navigate, especially given the broad range of potential visitors.

Approach

I personally contributed to the front-end development, focusing on responsive design, ensuring that the site worked flawlessly on both desktop and mobile devices. We implemented a grid system to display student portfolios, integrated multimedia content, and added features such as event registration and live updates. Our collaborative approach and iterative feedback process allowed us to refine the design and functionality, ensuring a smooth and polished user experience for all attendees.
